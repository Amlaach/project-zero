Method. Every run executed inside a pty capture (raw terminal screenshot + byte stream archived in benchmark_results/sweep3_2026-07-18/), strictly sequential, single session, same 7.16 GB GGUF, prompt "What is the capital of France?", 60 tokens, temperature 0. Four interleaved project-zero sentinel runs (t4 defaults) bracket all measurements at 2.73–3.12 tok/s (±7%) — cross-run differences inside that band are host noise, not signal. Fork: PrismML-Eng llama.cpp, prism branch, build b1-79697f2, -c 4096 -st (its default 262K context allocates a ~15 GB KV cache; its --no-conversation flag is rejected at runtime). SIMD and classifier axes exist only in project-zero. Ceiling = measured DRAM bandwidth ÷ model bytes/token (docs/architecture/CEILING_CALCULATION.md). project-zero includes the 2026-07-17 Q2_0 kernel optimization (+28%).
